# Shared Brief

Goal: Research AI tool market pain points, identify what makes products feel AI-made/vibe-coded, audit Cryonex for polish gaps, and upgrade the app toward a world-class human-crafted feel across platforms

Success criteria:
- Deliver a source-backed research summary covering:
  - complaints about AI tools and AI study tools from Reddit, forums, reviews, and articles
  - signals that make an app feel "AI-made", "templatey", or "vibe coded"
  - patterns that separate successful AI startups from failed ones
- Deliver a Cryonex-specific UI and UX audit with concrete examples from the current app.
- Deliver a QA report with severe functional, responsiveness, navigation, and empty-state failures.
- Translate the findings into implementation guidance for this repository, prioritizing polish, trust, clarity, performance, and cross-platform quality.

Constraints:
- Ground claims in current external sources where possible.
- Prefer artifacts with concrete examples over abstract advice.
- Do not revert unrelated local changes in the repo.
- Assume this turn should include both research synthesis and at least one meaningful implementation pass.

Target outcomes:
- The app should feel intentional, premium, and cohesive rather than auto-generated.
- The UI should reduce signs of clutter, inconsistency, fake complexity, and decorative overuse.
- Critical paths should feel production-ready on desktop and mobile.
