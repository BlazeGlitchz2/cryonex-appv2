# Cryonex AI Compliance Guide

_Updated: December 17, 2025_

## Risk Classification

Cryonex falls into the **Limited/Minimal Risk** category under the EU AI Act:

- General-purpose conversational AI assistant
- No biometric identification or scoring
- No manipulation of vulnerable groups
- Transparent AI-generated content disclosure

## EU AI Act Compliance (Dec 2025)

### ✅ Active Requirements

| Requirement               | Status       | Notes                            |
| ------------------------- | ------------ | -------------------------------- |
| Prohibited AI systems ban | ✅ Compliant | No banned practices implemented  |
| AI Literacy disclosure    | ✅ Compliant | Users know they interact with AI |
| GPAI transparency         | ✅ Compliant | Model sources disclosed          |

### Upcoming Requirements (Aug 2026+)

- High-risk AI system registration (not applicable to Cryonex)
- Conformity assessments for high-risk systems

## GDPR Compliance

### Data Handling

- ✅ **Minimization**: Only essential data collected
- ✅ **Transparency**: Clear privacy policy required
- ✅ **User Rights**: Access, rectification, deletion supported
- ✅ **Consent**: Explicit consent for optional features

### Recommended Disclosures

Add to Privacy Policy:

1. AI-generated responses may not be accurate
2. Conversations may be stored for service improvement
3. Third-party AI providers process queries
4. Users can delete conversation history

## Chat Interface Disclosure

Add this notice above the chat input:

```
"Responses are AI-generated and may contain errors. Verify important information."
```
